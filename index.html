Prayer Times

Know you the exact prayer time duration according to the coordinates taken from the browser. Also works on offline.

Highlited row makes easy to find the current prayer time. You may also check the remaining time of the next prayer time.

Multi language supported

Note: This is an open source project. Contribute to add more new features.

Upcoming features:

  • - Sahri and Iftar calendar.
  • - Random Hadith every waqt.
